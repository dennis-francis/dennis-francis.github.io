Ai Japanese Restaurant & Bar

Authentic Japanese Cuisine

World Famous sushi bar

Operating under Japanese Ownership since 1979

the Ai Japanese Restaurant is a South Pasadena local hotspot serving up your favorite Traditional Authentic Japanese Cuisine and dining. Our comfy atmosphere ambiance and booth seating is the perfect opportunity for you to enjoy a intimate dinner gathering with friends & family. Order sushi from the sushi bar and order a delicious cocktail and Mai Tai from the bar with live music entertainment on select nights. Online ordering is available or you can call for takeout.
